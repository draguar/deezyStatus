Privacy Policy for DeezyStatus

DeezyStatus is dedicated to safeguarding the privacy and security of its users' information. This Privacy Policy outlines the specific types of data collected by DeezyStatus, how it's utilized, and the measures taken to protect it.

Information Collection and Use

  • OAuth Workflow Authentication Tokens: DeezyStatus collects OAuth workflow authentication tokens during the app installation process. These tokens are necessary for interacting with Slack's API to update users' Slack statuses.
  • Slack User ID: DeezyStatus may also collect users' Slack user IDs to associate the OAuth tokens with specific Slack accounts.

Use of Information

The collected information is solely used for the purpose of updating users' Slack statuses in real-time based on the music they are currently listening to on Deezer. DeezyStatus does not share this information with any third parties or use it for any purposes other than the intended functionality of the app.

Data Security

DeezyStatus takes significant steps to ensure the security and confidentiality of users' OAuth tokens and Slack user IDs stored in Vercel's KV store:

  • Encrypted Storage: The data stored in Vercel's KV store is encrypted at rest, providing an additional layer of protection against unauthorized access.
  • Access Controls: Access to the KV store is strictly controlled and protected by strong authentication measures.
  • Compliance: Vercel's infrastructure complies with industry-standard security practices, ensuring data protection and compliance with relevant regulations.

Third-Party Services

DeezyStatus solely interacts with Slack's API using the OAuth tokens provided during installation. No other third-party services are involved in the handling or processing of user's personal data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

DeezyStatus may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect any changes in practices or legal requirements. Users will be informed of any revisions by publishing the updated policy on the DeezyStatus website.
