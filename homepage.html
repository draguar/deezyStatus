Welcome to DeezyStatus

DeezyStatus is a slack app that lets you automatically synchronize your Slack status with the track you're listening to on Deezer!

About DeezyStatus

To use DeezyStatus, you'll need to install the Firefox add-on DeezyTracker. Once you set up DeezyTracker and play music in Deezer, it will send the current track information to DeezyStatus to update your Slack status.
